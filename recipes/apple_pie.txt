Slow cooking channa masala
----------------------------
1. soak channa for 3 to 4 hrs
2. put it in cooker and add coriander powder and garam masala. add salt as needed.
3. 1 :3 cups of water
4. now, close the ip cooker and turn on for 6 to 7 hrs on medium heat. vent shud be in towards left or right.
5. Do some remix with powders and add it along with tadka in it and its done
